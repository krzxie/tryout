FOREWORD




WELCOME to Central Luzon Doctors' Hospital Educational
Institution!

CLDHEl is a non-sectarian basic and higher education
institution with programs for integrated K-12, undergraduate, and
graduate school. The vision, mission, and philosophy of the school
aim to provide a well-rounded education. Likewise, the objectives of
Basic Education are intended on providing fundamental livelihood
skills; strengthening moral, social, intellectual, and emotional
foundations for learners; educating learners on the careful use of
technology; developing problem solving, decision making, effective
communication and thinking skills; imbuing learners with positive
study and work habits; as well as inculcating appreciation and
pride for the Filipino culture and heritage. Finally, the objectives are
intended to prepare students for the more demanding requisites of
higher education.

Thank you for choosing CLDHEl as the instrument by which
you can achieve the goal you have set in your life. However, the
realization of your personal goals will greatly depend on how you
will respond to the tasks and challenges of formal education.

You are expected to abide by the provisions of this Student
Handbook which spells out your responsibilities as students of
CLDHEI.
